## The Problem: Why AFH Conversions Are Challenging

### Compliance Complexity

Washington State's Department of Social and Health Services (DSHS) has strict regulations for Adult Family Homes. These include:

- **Bedroom Requirements**: Minimum 80 square feet per room, egress windows, lockable doors
- **Bathroom Standards**: Specific toilet ratios, grab bars, non-slip surfaces
- **Safety Features**: Interconnected smoke detectors, fire extinguishers, temperature controls
- **Accessibility**: Door widths, ramps, handrails, and more

Manually checking a property against all these requirements can take days or weeks. One missed detail can result in a failed inspection, delaying licensing and costing thousands in additional renovations.

### Cost Uncertainty

Without proper planning, renovation costs can spiral out of control. Property owners often discover compliance issues during inspection, leading to expensive emergency fixes. Budget overruns of 20-30% are common.

### Time Constraints

The traditional process involves:
1. Manual property assessment
2. Researching DSHS regulations
3. Creating renovation plans
4. Getting contractor quotes
5. Performing renovations
6. Hoping everything passes inspection

This can take 3-6 months or longer.

## The Solution: AFH Renovator Pro

AFH Renovator Pro automates the entire planning process, turning weeks of work into minutes.

### How It Works

**Step 1: Property Input**
Simply enter an MLS number or property address. The tool integrates with NWMLS Matrix to fetch comprehensive property data, including square footage, room dimensions, floor plans, and more.

**Step 2: Automatic Analysis**
The application analyzes the property against all relevant DSHS regulations (WAC 388-76 series), identifying compliance issues automatically.

**Step 3: Plan Generation**
Based on the analysis, AFH Renovator Pro generates a customized renovation plan with:
